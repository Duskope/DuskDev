About DuskDev

One-man show

My name is Noah. I am the sole-operator of DuskDev. I have been coding with HTML, CSS, and JS since I was 18. At the start of my journey my goal was to be a part of a bigger company, dabbling in react.js, and just wanted a job as a web developer. But I realized the freedom I could have working for myself, and the opportunity to do things my way, being able to help small businesses without a big corp sucking work out of developers for little pay, while charging clients out the ass for standard websites, while the big guys fill their pockets. By starting my LLC, I run my company how I please, competing with the bigger companies at prices that they can't beat, with quality work that matches or typically exceeds them.

Quality over quantity

Operating on only a Linux-powered laptop, DuskDev is a one-man show, ensuring a personalized touch to every project. Utilizing industry-standard tools such as Visual Studio Code and GitHub, combined with testing on Google Chrome, DuskDev guarantees websites that not only meet but exceed expectations. My streamlined approach allows to keep costs low without compromising on the quality of the product.

While based in Eastern North Carolina, DuskDev LLC extends its services beyond geographical boundaries. Thanks to the virtual nature of operations, DuskDev can cater to clients nationwide, offering them the same level of dedication and expertise that defines our local services.

These websites are created one at a time, so I can concentrate my focus on making them exactly how the client wants it without getting off-track.

Looking out for small businesses

Ever noticed how big web development companies charge an arm and a leg for what seems like a basic website? I did too. I have seen companies pay around $30k on a single sloppy website, just because it was from a big company. The internet is a vast space filled with endless possibilities, and everyone deserves a slice of that digital pie without draining their budget. This is where DuskDev shines, offering quality that matches or exceeds the big players, all at a price that makes sense.

What does "DuskDev" mean?

Why the name "DuskDev"? When I was a younger, I wanted a unique online name that no one else had. I came up with "Duskope". It doesn't really mean anything, but no one else had it. That's what I used as my gamertag on many videogames or websites. So I just crossed Duskope and Developer to get DuskDev.
