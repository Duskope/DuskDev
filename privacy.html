Last Updated: 01/14/24

Welcome to DuskDev LLC. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or submit your contact information through our contact form. Information We Collect

Contact Information

When you fill out our contact form, we collect the following contact information:

We collect and use your contact information for the sole purpose of responding to your inquiries, providing information about our services, and maintaining communication related to potential or ongoing projects.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your contact information to third parties without your explicit consent. Your information is used exclusively for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy.

We retain the contact information you provide through our contact form for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. If you wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us at duskopedev@gmail.com.

We take reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality and security of your contact information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your contact information. If you would like to exercise any of these rights or have questions about the information we hold, please contact us at duskopedev@gmail.com.

Consent

By submitting your contact information through our contact form, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact DuskDev

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at duskopedev@gmail.com. Thank you for choosing DuskDev LLC. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting and safeguarding your contact information.
